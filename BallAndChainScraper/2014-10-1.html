Mission Statement

A new beer and meatballery has arrived to the Cahuenga corridor in the heart of Hollywood!

The restaurant features a gourmet meatball centric menu with 20 rotating California craft beers on tap and over 100 unique beers by the bottle!

Ball & Chain brings an ever-changing menu with customizable meatballs available in ways such as sliders, a la carte, atop a bed of greens, or on a hoagie. As well as unexpected variations such as vegan, turducken, elk and surprising daily specials!

Owner Marc Kreiner of the Morrison and designer/partner Kristofer Keith created a dark and intimate setting with an edgy industrial feel, making the venue an enjoyable hotspot both day and night.
